The Palette Restaurant

Experience culinary artistry at The Palette, where each dish is a masterpiece of color and flavor. Our chefs transform locally-sourced ingredients into vibrant, contemporary creations.

Open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Featuring an extensive wine list and craft cocktail menu with artistic presentations.

The Chroma Spa

Rejuvenate your senses in our serene wellness sanctuary. Inspired by color therapy, each treatment room offers a unique chromatic experience designed to restore balance and vitality.

Our holistic approach combines traditional techniques with modern wellness practices in an ambiance of dim lighting, textured stones, and essential oils.

  • Color therapy treatments
  • Steam room & sauna
  • Massage & body treatments
  • Private meditation sessions
